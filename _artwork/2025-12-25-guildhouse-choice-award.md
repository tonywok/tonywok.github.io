I was recently nominated in the annual OS Guildhouse Choice Awards in the category of Freedom of Expression. I was very flattered -- even more so when I was asked to put together a prize card for the "Best Throwback Playset".

We workshopped a couple ideas but ultimately landed on a 90s throw-back. So I reached for the classic Bevis and Butthead sitting on the couch paired with red and blue elemental blasts emitting from the TV antenna.

Congrats to the winner!
