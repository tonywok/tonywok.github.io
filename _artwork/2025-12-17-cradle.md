Cradles are expensive cards these days and I needed a few for a silly deck I was planning to play at a Retromancers' meetup.

I was decompressing at the end of the night and started making a super rough color wash thinking I'd approximate some very quick cradles to use.

I got goine on one and never went back to the others.

I referenced the original for a while but eventually just started going a new direction.
