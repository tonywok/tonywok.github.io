The name of our local Premodern Magic club is Retromancers, so naturally we have a bajillion of them.

It's a shame the card is truly terrible. That said, thankfully there's a decent number of cards that produce 3/3 tokens, so they aren't completely useless.

Anyway, Travis, a local member and artist, has started to collect the silly things, so I tried my hand at making one for him.

It's meant to be a rancor. The fire doesn't _really_ make sense, but I thought it looked cool on the red.
